Frequent Questions

What is Direct Indexing?

It's a strategy used by wealthy investors to replicate an index (like the S&P 500) by buying the stocks directly, rather than buying a fund. This avoids the 38% Deemed Disposal Tax.

Do you hold my money?

Never. Your assets sit in your own Interactive Brokers or Alpaca account.

Why not a Pension?

Pensions are for age 60+. This is for your 'Bridge' years (House, Wedding, Early Retirement).

Is it really as passive as an ETF?

Yes. We built it for "Lazy Investing." You don't need spreadsheets. When you deposit funds, our engine uses Inflow Rebalancingâ€”it automatically calculates which stocks are underweight and buys only those. You get index tracking without doing the math.

How do I sell? Do I have to click 50 times?

No. You use our "One-Click Withdraw." You simply type the amount you need (e.g., â‚¬5,000). The engine automatically sells the correct fraction of each stock to maintain your portfolio balance, executing all trades instantly.

What happens if a company drops out of the criteria?

We remove it to keep your index pure.

If a stock no longer meets the criteria (e.g., it falls out of the Top 30), our engine flags it for removal. We prioritize selling it when it is at a loss (to lower your tax bill). If it is at a gain, we may phase it out slowly to avoid an unnecessary tax bill.

Are the taxes complicated to file?

We automate the math.

Calculating Capital Gains Tax (CGT) manually is painful. That's why we built the Tax Hub. At the end of the year, you can download a "Revenue-Ready" PDF Report. It gives you the exact figures to copy-paste into your Revenue Form CG1. We track every trade, dividend, and currency conversion for you.
